The Ethos

Timing, treated as strategy

Moon & Money is the work of Luna Wilde, a practice that treats the lunar calendar as the oldest scheduling system we have, and applies it to money with discipline.

The Philosophy

Not mysticism. Method.

For centuries, work was scheduled to the lunar calendar because timing changed the result. Money is no different. There is a time to begin: open the account, set the transfer. A time to assess: review what is working. And a time to cut what quietly costs you.

Read your Moon sign as a profile of financial temperament, how you relate to security, risk and worth, and money becomes a system you run, not a fight with yourself. Disciplined timing, modern strategy, presented with restraint.

The Practice

Three movements

I

Begin

The new moon. Make the move you keep deferring: open the account, set the transfer, place the order.

II

Assess

The full moon. Review the position with clear eyes. Keep what performs; name what does not.

III

Refine

The waning moon. Close the leak, cancel the drain, reallocate. Make room for the next cycle.
