The contest begins on [START DATE] and runs on an ongoing basis until the Sponsor announces its end, with no fewer than thirty (30) days’ notice posted at moonandmoney.ca/scratch/rules. Entries received after the announced end date will not be eligible.

4. How to enter

There are two ways to enter, and both have equal chances of winning.

Method A — The card. Obtain a Moon & Money scratch card (distributed free, without obligation, in various locations across Canada). Scratch the coating to reveal the QR code and unique code printed beneath. Scan the QR code or visit moonandmoney.ca/scratch and enter the code. The site will display your lunar reading and indicate whether your card is a winning entry.

Method B — Free entry by mail. Mail a clearly legible, hand-written request including your full name, mailing address, date of birth, email address, and the phrase “Moon & Money Lunar Lottery — Free Entry” to:

5. Prizes

Each winning entry receives one (1) complimentary Money Chart (personalized astrological-economic report, retail value $55 CAD), delivered as a digital PDF to the winner’s email address.

The Sponsor reserves the right to substitute a prize of equal or greater value if the original prize becomes unavailable.

6. Odds of winning

Approximately one (1) in fifty (50) entries is a winning entry. Final odds depend on the total number of entries received during the contest period.

7. Winner notification

Winners are notified at the moment of entry: the moonandmoney.ca/scratch page will display a winning message. Winners must complete the steps in Section 8 below to claim their prize.

8. Skill-testing question and claim procedure

To be declared a winner and receive a prize, an entrant must, within thirty (30) days of notification:

(a) Correctly answer, unaided, a time-limited mathematical skill-testing question presented on the claim page. The question will involve a combination of addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division (for example: “(12 + 8) × 3 − 6 = ?”);

(b) Provide a valid email address for delivery of the digital prize;

(c) Provide their full name, date of birth, and city/province for verification of eligibility;

(d) Sign and return, electronically, a declaration confirming compliance with these Rules and a release of liability.

If a selected entrant fails to complete any of the above within thirty (30) days, the entry will be forfeited and the prize will be reassigned to the next entry in the random draw.
