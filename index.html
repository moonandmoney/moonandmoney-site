The Premise

Money has a rhythm. Most people were simply never taught to read it.

Moon & Money takes the oldest timing system on record, the lunar calendar, and applies it as a considered financial practice. Not mysticism. Method. A time to begin, a time to build, a time to assess, and a time to cut what no longer earns its place.

Each Friday, a new reading lands. A growing library of guides, calendars and tools turns timing into a strategy you can act on.
